Get it

Get it in three steps

Start in your browser. Nothing to install.

1

Open claude.ai/code in your browser. Nothing to install.

2

Or download the desktop app at claude.ai/download. Mac and Windows.

3

Free at claude.ai, upgrade to Pro about $20 a month. You subscribe yourself.

One switch: in claude.ai Settings, then Privacy, turn off “Help improve Claude”. 10 seconds.

Advanced: the terminal, most people never need this

Tech-minded only. On Mac or Linux, run curl -fsSL https://claude.ai/install.sh | bash. On Windows PowerShell, run irm https://claude.ai/install.ps1 | iex. Then type claude and press Enter.
